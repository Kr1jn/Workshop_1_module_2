Mark Zuckerberg

What is Mark Zuckerberg famous for?


Mark Zuckerberg is famous for being the cofounder and chief executive officer of Facebook, the world's largest social network Web site.
He founded the service in 2004 while he was at Harvard University with four of his fellow students.

How much cash does Mark Zuckerberg have?

Mark Zuckerberg is one of the most powerful and wealthiest figures in the world.
He has a net worth of $64.7 billion, according to Forbes, and spends much of it on charity

What is the family background of Mark Zuckerberg?

Early life. Mark Elliot Zuckerberg was born in White Plains, New York, on May 14, 1984, the son of psychiatrist Karen (née Kempner) and dentist Edward Zuckerberg. He and his three sisters (Arielle, businesswoman Randi, and writer Donna) were raised in a Reform Jewish household in Dobbs Ferry, New York.
